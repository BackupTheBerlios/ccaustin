The Building Momentum CD
The CD is being distributed in conjunction with the Building Momentum campaign at Calvary Chapel of Austin. It contains audio messages, notes and study questions that will help to educate you about the biblical principles of giving. It is our hope that this material will build a solid foundation and strengthen your knowledge on this important topic.

Vision
 The vision of Calvary Chapel of Austin for this CD is for you to realize that giving of yourself, including financial resources, is part of God's plan for all believers. It is our hope that when finished with the content that you will have a better understanding and insight into what giving as a Christian means.


Purpose
 Our purpose, for you, in distributing the Building Momentum CD is to equip you with the information necessary to understand the biblical principles of giving. With a solid understanding of biblical giving, each person can then make an informed decision, guided by the Holy Spirit and prayer, as to what role this plays in their life.
