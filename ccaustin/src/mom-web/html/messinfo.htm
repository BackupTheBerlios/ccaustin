Messages
 The included messages will better help you to understand the biblical principles of giving. Many people have questions about what the Bible has to say on the topic of giving. Our hope is that by listening to these topical messages that you can understand and apply the principles that are being taught. After listening to the messages you should be able to understand some of the following principles:

  • What does the bible says about giving?
  • Do you feel compelled to give? Or do you give out of your heart’s desire?
  • Have you given yourself to the Lord? Is He truly your master? If not, what is holding you back, and why?
  • Who owns your money? What does being a faithful steward mean to you?
  • Do you really trust God to meet your needs? If not, why?
  • Does the topic of money being taught in church bother you? Why? Are you being obedient in this area of your Christian life?
  • Do you give regularly? If so, what is your plan? If not, will you commit to giving regularly?
  • Why should we give to our local church?
  • Do you see giving as worship?
  • How much should I give?
  • What do you expect God to do in response to your giving?
