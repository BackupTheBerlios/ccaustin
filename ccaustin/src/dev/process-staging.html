Staging Process

The staging process allows changes made and commited to the CVS source control server for publishing to be built for others to see. We offer the staging process on an hourly basis, at the top of the hour. Therefore, by :05 after the hour (approx), your changes that were commited to CVS will be seen for review. You may use this to verify your changes (all files were added/updated to CVS, etc) and also to send an email to someone that requested a fix, so they may see the changes and review the verbage for event descriptions, etc.

In the future, we will enable content editors to review changes and mark them as publishable, thereby reducing mistakes that tend to be more common from automatic publishing (and when you have more content generators than actual web dev staff - all your ministry leaders, etc.).
