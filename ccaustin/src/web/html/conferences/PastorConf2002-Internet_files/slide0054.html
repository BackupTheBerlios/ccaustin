Intro to HCI (con’t)
Here are some hints:
l Assume that people are only going to read the headlines
l Make reading accessible in small chunks
l Keep copy blocks small
l Use small images as visual cues to deliver key
messages.
l Use color and bold type to drive eyes to the most
important information.
l Keep your most important messages "above the fold."
People may or may not scroll down.
l Use photos of people. People are attracted to images of
other people
