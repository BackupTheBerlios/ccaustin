Intro to HCI
The research found that people:
l Processed information based on the hierarchy
presented.
l Used headlines, photos, and graphic images as entry
points.
l Processed more headlines than photos.
l Typically processed a page from right to left; sometimes
in a sideways U-pattern (refuting the old left to right, top
to bottom thinking).
l Typically did not read "below the fold“ (don’t scroll down)
l Did not necessarily respond better to bigger images.
l Were particularly attracted to images of people
